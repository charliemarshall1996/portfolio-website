Core Scores

The Core Scores provide the overview of your website analysis. They are split into four categories: Accessibility, Best Practices, Performance, Search Engine Optimization (SEO). Each of these four categories are important for making your website gets visitors and those visitors stay on your site. The longer they are on your site, the more likely those visitors are to convert into paying customers.

These scores represent a weighted average of a series of metrics which identify where your website is performing well, but more importantly, where it is performing poorly. Based on analysis of these metrics, we should be able to identify problem areas, which allow us to define actionable steps which can be taken to improve the traffic of your website, leading to more customers.
