Discovery Call

We will begin the journey to building your ultimate web app with a discovery call. This call will allow me to better understand your desires for your application, and allow you ask any burning questions you may have about the journey ahead.

Project Definition

Based on the outcome of the discovery call, I will create a project definition document. This will ensure we all understand what is within the scope of the project, and what will be in the end product.

Conceptualization

Now it's time to get to work!

In the conceptualization stage, I will design and provide mockups of the end product. It is here where you'll be able to see what the product will look like, including any portals, and database schematics.

Initial Build

Once you're happy with the concept, I will start the initial build. Here is where I will create the core functionality of the project. It will ensure for a solid foundation to build upon, when it comes to fine-tuning your application.

Iterative Development

Once the foundations of the product are built, I get to work on the granular details. This is where we get to fine-tune the product to truly be the web app you envisioned.

Deployment

Once you are happy with the project, the product is now ready for deployment. It is now ready to show to the world!
